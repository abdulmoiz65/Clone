IE LAW SCHOOL CELEBRATES THE GRADUATION OF OVER 250 MASTER’S STUDENTS
The ceremony, which also included several awards for students and faculty, featured emotional and inspiring moments.
On Friday, July 14th, more than 250 master’s students marked the end of their academic journey at IE Law School with its seventh postgraduate ceremony held at the IE Tower.
The event brought together faculty, students, professors, and friends to celebrate the talents of graduates from the following programs: Master in Legal Practice, Master in International Legal Studies, Master in Laws (LL.M.), Master in Global Corporate Compliance, Master in Taxation, Doble Máster en Abogacía y Asesoría Jurídica de Empresas, and the Doble Máster en Asesoría Jurídica Fiscal de PwC.
